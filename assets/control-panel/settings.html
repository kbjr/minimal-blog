Blog Title / Description

The title and description of your blog will be displayed in the title bar of visitors' browsers, as well as prominently on the main webpage. They should describe the blog as a whole, and what kind of content people can expect to find here.

Content Language

The content language will be presented in metadata to your visitors' browsers or other clients, to inform them of what language to expect content on the site to be written in.

Copyright Notice

The copyright notice text will be displayed alongside any content on the page, to identify the owner of the website's contents.

Favicon

A Favicon is an icon image that is often displayed alongside your page, such as in the title bar of a web browser. They are typically fairly small and have square dimensions.
